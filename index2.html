Casserly Engineering and Dispute Resolution is a small consultancy based in county Sligo. From an engineering perspective the company provides engineering consultancy, structural reports, planning and building compliance, safety consultancy, contract and tendering, and several other engineering related services.

The company also specialises in dispute resolution and provides mediation, conciliation, adjudication, arbitration, and similar services in this area.
